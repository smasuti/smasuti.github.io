Biography

I am researcher at Earth observatory of Singapore. In my PhD, I studied the transient creep of olivine and olivine bearing rocks using data from laboratory experiments. I applied the laboratory knowledge to study postseismic deformation following the 2012 Mw 8.6 Indian Ocean earthquake.

Interests

  • Rheology of olivine
  • Postseismic deformation
  • Earthquake physics
  • Numerical modeling
  • MCMC techniques
